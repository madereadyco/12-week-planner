Before you start

What is the 12 Week Year — and why 12 weeks, not 12 months?

You don't need to have read the book to use this. Here's everything you need to know.

📐
A year of urgency, compressed into 12 weeks

The 12 Week Year is an execution framework from the book of the same name by Brian Moran and Michael Lennington. The core idea: annual goals lose urgency by month three. Treating 12 weeks as if it were a full year — with a real deadline, not a vague annual target — keeps every week feeling like it matters.

It isn't about doing more. It's about doing fewer things, consistently, and measuring whether you actually did them — not just whether you hit the end result.

The five components — and where each lives in the sheet
🌅 Vision
A vivid picture of what success looks like in 12 weeks — lives in 🎯 Vision & Goals
🥇 Goals (max 3)
No more than 3 goals per cycle — more dilutes focus — set in 🎯 Vision & Goals
🗓️ Weekly tactics
Specific actions for each of the 12 weeks, broken down in 🗓️ Weekly Plan
📊 Execution scoring
A weekly score of tasks completed vs planned — calculated automatically in 📊 Scorecard
⏳ Buffer week
A 13th week for rest, review, and resetting — built into 🗓️ Weekly Plan and 🪞 Review

Questions

Things people ask before buying

Do I need to have read the 12 Week Year book?+

No. The "What is the 12 Week Year?" section above explains everything you need — vision, 3-goal focus, weekly tactics, execution scoring, and the buffer week. The sheet itself guides you through each step the first time you open it.

Can I use this for both personal and work goals?+

Yes — this was a specific design choice. The Planning Mode setting and category dropdown cover fitness, habits, learning, side projects, career, sales targets, and team OKRs in one flexible structure. Many buyers run one personal goal and two work goals in the same cycle.

What happens after 12 weeks — do I need to buy it again?+

No. Duplicate the relevant tabs (Weekly Plan, Scorecard, Habit Tracker) for your next cycle, or simply update the cycle start date in Vision & Goals and clear last cycle's data. One purchase covers every 12-week cycle you ever run.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use this?+

None at all. You need a free Google account and the ability to tick a checkbox. The ReadMe PDF and 90-second Loom video walk you through every step. Most buyers are set up and planning their first week in under 10 minutes.

Does the Pro version require an API key or subscription?+

No. All you need is a free Google account. The AI features in MR12W Pro — Weekly Coach, Cycle Insight, Off-Track Alerts — run on Made Ready's own infrastructure, covered by your one-time purchase. No Anthropic account, no Claude subscription.

I already bought Standard. How do I upgrade to Pro?+

Search "Made Ready 12-Week Planner Pro Upgrade" in the Made Ready Co Etsy shop — listed at £10 for existing Standard customers. Your existing goals, weekly plan, and scorecard history copy across in about two minutes.

Does this work on iPhone or Android?+

Yes — Google Sheets has full iOS and Android apps. The habit tracker and scorecard are fully usable on mobile, which most buyers use for the daily habit check-ins. We recommend doing the initial weekly planning on a laptop or desktop.

Will this work with Microsoft Excel?+

Made Ready 12-Week Planner is built specifically for Google Sheets and uses Google's Apps Script for all automation — the auto-scoring, the menu, and the AI features. It is not compatible with Microsoft Excel. A free Google account is all you need.
